NYCLock: Peace of mind for the urban commuter.

NYCLock is a partnership between the Department of Transportation and design studio Alter Works. An urban practice with prior community projects, they were able to create a bike locking system which facilitates individual urban transport.

Ease of Access: All you need is a smartphone or your NYC ID!

Becoming part of the NYCLock community is as easy as registering here, and downloading the official app or linking your NYC ID to the service, which can be done through their website.

  • Get the app
  • Register your vehicle
  • Ride to an NYCLock and lock it with the app!


24/7 Support & Safety

Being a NYC DOT effort, you can rest assured in your vehicles security. If there happens to be a technical problem, you can request immediate assistance from our online service and helpful staff.
